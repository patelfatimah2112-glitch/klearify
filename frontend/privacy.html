Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 9, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Klearify. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website at klearify.vercel.app.

Please read this policy carefully. If you disagree with its terms, please stop using our website.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide our service:

  • Concepts and topics you enter for explanation
  • Your selected interests and language preferences
  • Usage data such as number of explanations generated
  • If you sign up, your email address and name

We do NOT collect or store sensitive personal data like passwords in plain text, payment information, or location data.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information only to:

  • Generate personalized explanations for you
  • Save your explanation history (if logged in)
  • Improve our AI explanations and service quality
  • Send you updates about Klearify (only if you subscribe)

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. We use Supabase for database storage, which is SOC 2 compliant and follows strict security standards.

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to any third parties.

5. Third Party Services

We use the following third party services to operate Klearify:

  • Groq API — for generating AI explanations
  • Vercel — for hosting our website
  • Render — for hosting our backend server
  • Supabase — for database and authentication

Each of these services has their own privacy policy and data protection measures.

6. Cookies

We use local storage (similar to cookies) to save your preferences like dark/light mode and daily credit usage. This data stays on your device and is not sent to our servers.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data at any time
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt out of any marketing communications
  • Know what data we hold about you

8. Children's Privacy

Klearify is designed to be safe for students of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Your continued use of Klearify after any changes means you accept the updated policy.
